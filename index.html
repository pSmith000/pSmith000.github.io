PROJECTS

Skyline
Shredder

This is a third-person momentum based parkour game that has you racing around a city completing
time trials in the fastest time possible. I worked on the base parkour movement mechanics, the
grinding system, the radio system, the entirety of the custom soundtrack, and was the lead designer
on the project.

Paper
Blaster

This is an arcade style game playing as a paper airplane. You move with boosters on the back of the
airplane while dodging and shooting the paper "asteroids" that float around. There are also stickers
that chase you down that you must destroy using either your regular fire rate or with powerups. I
worked on the player movement, the soundtrack, and the powerups.
